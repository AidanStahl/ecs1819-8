About Norman Borlaug:

Norman Borlaug was a scientist who had a big influence on the green revolution also known as the third agricultural revolution. The third agricultural revolution was the revolution that introduced chemical pestasides and Geneticaly modified seeds. Because of borlaugs introduction of his new technology this allowed for people who lived in countries and places that were lacking in production of food to start producing more of a surplus of food which allowed for less starvation leading to the title of the man who saved a billion lives as Borlaug is knowen as. Also the introduction of sustanable agriculture which is a more efficient method of agriculture was introduced. This method of agriculture included the use of less chemicals and pestasides and drip irrigation so you can give the plant just the right amount of water so you do not waste any extra water. Also this led to a decreace in prices of food at the store because more of it could be produced. All this things sparked because of the innovations by Norman Borlaug.
